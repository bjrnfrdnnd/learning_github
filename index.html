My Story Thus Far...

Was born some time ago.
Managed to get my high school diploma (Abitur).
Got my PhD in Physics (computational theoretical biophysics).
Lived some years abroad (in beautiful France and s(t)unningly gorgeous Spain).
Worked as a PostDoc/Interim Assistant Professor for some German Universities.
Discovered funny things about ears and growing polymers.
Published some papers about that.
Stopped Working in Academia.

The Saga Continues...
